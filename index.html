01

Artís

Artís is a coffee roastary and café in San Francisco that roasts small batches to sustain peak freshness. They came to our team for web and video help. We delved into the brand, considered their core values, rally cry, customer needs, and digital experience.

02

Brand

The target demographics for Artís are Persons of Particulars: the Gourmet, the Cultural Explorer, and the Connesiuer/Craft Enthusiast.

The archetypes that this brand embodies are the Explorer, Creator, and the Storyteller. Artís is about freshness and flavor. At it's core, this brand is about seizing the moment since coffee peaks in flavor 24-48 hours after its roasted.

03

Web and Copywriting

The typical customer for Artís is interested in what they are purchasing and especially in what they are consuming. They enjoy learning, so we gave them not only information about the café and how to roast, but also information about the history of the coffee and its origins. The voice for Artís is casual and educated. It was important to balance the information presented with a reminder of how it's relevant to your cup. So for example: in the How to Brew sections, we explain how to make coffee using a Chemex, but also cover why you would want to (in case you were curious, it yields a cup with a light body and clean taste).

04

Flavor Profile


Coffee is such a commonplace drink that it can be strange to think about it like wine, with regions and processing affecting the flavor complexities. It can be strange to start thinking about describing the way coffee tastes, or which coffee you like. We included a flavor profile quiz to help non-coneisseurs talk about the flavors they enjoyed in coffee and those they didn't. The quiz yields results about the region and the roast that you might like. It was named by SF Weekly as one of the best they've seen.
